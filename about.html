About Me

Olivia Jackson is always ready and prepared to give customers her full attention regarding their real estate needs. To do this she stays up to date on the latest real estate trends, continuing education and developments in her local real estate market. Olivia brings 18 yrs experience helping Sellers, Buyers and Investors with their Real Estate needs along with a great attitude, tenacity, and attentiveness to the unique needs of her customers. It's not just about putting in a lot of time with Olivia, it's all about working smart, putting in the right amount of time and doing what it takes to help her customers close successfully. Olivia uses her expertise and foresight to proactively address details upfront and before issues arise. However, should they arise, it’s assuring to know Olivia will work creatively finding solutions to solve them. These 4 words best describe Olivia’s attitude when working with customers "Olivia gets it done!" this has always been Olivia's vision and you can rest assured she will bring the same energy when negotiating for you. Originally from Somerville, New Jersey, Olivia is dedicated to her career in real estate. When she isn’t glued to her computer, Olivia enjoys walking, volunteering, traveling and spending time with family and friends.
